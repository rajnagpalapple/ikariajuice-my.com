Here is a list of major Ikaria Juice Vitamins & Ingredients along with their benefits: 

1

Fucoxanthin

Fucoxanthin is a type of carotenoid found mainly in brown seaweed. It's known for its antioxidant properties and its potential to promote weight loss by increasing the metabolic rate and reducing abdominal fat.
2

Capsaicin

Capsaicin is the compound that gives chili peppers their heat. It's been shown to boost metabolism, reduce hunger, and increase fat burning, making it a popular ingredient in weight loss supplements.
3

Dandelion

Dandelion is a plant that is rich in fiber and low in calories, making it a good choice for weight loss. It's also packed with vitamins and minerals and may help reduce inflammation and regulate blood sugar levels.
4

Silymarin

Silymarin, an active ingredient in milk thistle, is known for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. It's been suggested that it may help with weight loss by improving liver function, which plays a crucial role in metabolism and detoxification.
5

Panax Ginseng

Panax Ginseng is a plant that has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. It's believed to boost energy levels, reduce stress, and help with weight loss by regulating metabolism.
6

Resveratrol

Resveratrol is a plant compound that acts as an antioxidant. It's found in grapes, berries, and peanuts. Studies suggest that it may help with weight loss by increasing metabolism and muscle endurance.
7

Citrus Pectin

Citrus Pectin is a type of fiber found in fruits and vegetables. It's known for its ability to promote feelings of fullness, which can help reduce calorie intake and contribute to weight loss.
8

EGCG (Epigallocatechin Gallate)

EGCG (Epigallocatechin Gallate) is a type of catechin found in green tea. It's been shown to boost metabolism and increase fat burning, making it a popular ingredient in weight loss supplements.
9

Bioperine

Bioperine is a patented form of black pepper extract that is often added to supplements to enhance the absorption of other nutrients.
10

Vitamin C

Vitamin C is a vital nutrient for health. It's an antioxidant that protects the body from damage by free radicals. It's also involved in many body functions, including the production of collagen, absorption of iron, the immune system, wound healing, and the maintenance of cartilage, bones, and teeth.
